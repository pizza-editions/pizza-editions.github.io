How to Play Subway Surfers New York

In Subway Surfers New York, you surf in the Big Apple as Jake! Use keys to jump, slide, or dodge taxis. Grab coins to win big. Can you escape in this endless runner game?

  • Up Arrow: Jump over bins.
  • Down Arrow: Slide under signs.
  • Left/Right Arrow: Switch lanes.
  • Spacebar: Use hoverboard.
  • R: Restart if you crash.
  • ESC: Pause or open the menu.

Jump, surf, and win in this super fun endless runner game! Play now on Pizza Edition!

Description of Subway Surfers New York

Subway Surfers New York is an awesome endless runner game where you play as Jake or characters like Tony, using arrow keys to jump, slide, or dodge taxis and bins in New York’s subways. Collect coins and tokens for power-ups like jetpacks or the Red Velvet hoverboard. The game’s city-themed art and hip music make every run a blast. This endless runner game is perfect for kids who love world tours and fast chases!

Kids love this game because it’s super exciting to surf through New York and unlock cool characters. The taxi traps and fun power-ups keep you running to beat your score and be a subway star. Can you dash through the city? Try Subway Surfers New York now and zoom into endless runner fun!

Game Creator

Subway Surfers New York was made by Kiloo and SYBO Games, a team that makes fun world tour endless runner games for kids.

FAQ about Subway Surfers New York

What’s new in New York?
You surf New York with taxis and bins! Unlock Tony or the Red Velvet board!

What’s so special about this game?
The Big Apple theme is super fun! City art and hip tunes make every run a cool adventure!

How do I dodge taxis?
Use Up Arrow to jump, Down Arrow to slide, and Left/Right Arrows to move. Time it right!

Who made Subway Surfers New York?
Kiloo and SYBO Games created it. They make fun world tour games!

Is Subway Surfers New York multiplayer?
No, it’s single-player, but you can share scores with friends online!

What’s different from Subway Surfers?
New York has city taxis and art, while the main game has mixed cities. Both are awesome!
