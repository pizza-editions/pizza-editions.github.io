How to Play Iron Snout

In Iron Snout, you’re a tough pig fighting waves of wolves! Punch, kick, and dodge to beat enemies. Grab weapons like axes for extra power. Can you survive the wolf attack in this fighting game?

  • Arrow Keys or WASD: Punch, kick, or dodge left, right, up, or down.
  • Spacebar: Use special moves or grab weapons.
  • R: Restart if you get hit too much.
  • ESC: Pause or open the menu.

Fight, dodge, and win in this super fun fighting game! Play now on The Pizza Edition!

Description of Iron Snout

Iron Snout is an exciting fighting game where you play as a brave pig facing off against endless wolves. Use punches, kicks, and flips to knock out enemies, and grab weapons like knives or rockets to hit harder. Each wave of wolves gets faster and trickier, with some throwing axes or riding bikes, so you need quick reflexes to stay alive. The game’s funny cartoon style and wild action make every fight a blast. This fighting game is great for kids who love fast-paced battles and silly fun!

Kids love this game because it’s super fast and makes you feel like a ninja pig. You’ll want to keep fighting to beat your high score and take down more wolves. Can you survive the wolf invasion? Try Iron Snout now and show your fighting game skills!
