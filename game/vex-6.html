How to Play Vex 6

In Vex 6, you dash as a stickman! Use keys to dodge spikes and swim past traps. Reach the end to win. Can you escape in this platformer game?

  • WASD/Arrow Keys: Move, jump, or slide.
  • S/Down Arrow: Enter level or slide.
  • R: Restart if you die.
  • ESC: Pause or open the menu.

Description of Vex 6

Vex 6 is an awesome platformer game where you control a stickman, using WASD or arrow keys to run, jump, slide, or swim through nine acts and nine hard acts packed with cannons, razors, and spikes. Reach the green portal to finish, and collect coins to unlock 64 skins. The game’s sharp 2D graphics and cool music make every level a thrill. This platformer game is perfect for kids who love parkour and challenges!
