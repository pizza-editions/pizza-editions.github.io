How to Play Temple of Boom

In Temple of Boom, you fight in a temple! Use keys to jump and shoot monsters. Grab coins to win. Can you battle big in this action game?

  • Arrow Keys: Move or jump.
  • Z: Shoot gun.
  • X: Switch weapons.
  • R: Restart if you lose.
  • ESC: Pause or open the menu.

Jump, shoot, and win in this super fun action game! Play now on Pizza Edition!

Description of Temple of Boom

Temple of Boom is an awesome action game where you play as an explorer, using arrow keys to jump and Z to shoot monsters in a wild temple. Pick up coins and weapons like shotguns, and use X to switch them to keep fighting. Unlock new levels or heroes with wins. The game’s retro 2D art and spooky sounds make every fight a thrill. This action game is perfect for kids who love battles and temples!

Kids love this game because it’s super exciting to shoot monsters and find coins. The tricky temples and cool weapons keep you fighting to win every level and be a temple champ. Can you clear the temple? Try Temple of Boom now and blast into action game fun!

Game Creator
