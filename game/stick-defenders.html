How to Play Stick Defenders

In Stick Defenders, you merge stickmen to fight enemies! Use your mouse to combine fighters and stop waves. Protect your base to win. Can you defend in this action game?

  • Mouse Click: Merge stickmen.
  • Drag: Move fighters to merge.
  • R: Restart if you lose.
  • ESC: Pause or open the menu.

Merge, fight, and win in this super fun action game! Play now on The Pizza Edition!

Description of Stick Defenders

Stick Defenders is an awesome action game where you use your mouse to merge identical stickmen, creating stronger fighters to protect your base from enemy waves. Combine gunmen to boost their power, upgrade walls, and use bonuses like air strikes. Spin the wheel for extra items to help you win. The game’s cool 2D art and battle sounds make every fight exciting. This action game is perfect for kids who love stickman battles and strategy!

Kids love this game because it’s super fun to merge stickmen and blast enemies. The big fights and cool upgrades keep you defending to save your base and be a stickman hero. Can you stop the waves? Try Stick Defenders now and charge into action game fun!

FAQ about Stick Defenders

How many fighter types are there?
You can merge six types of fighters! Each one’s stronger, like snipers or rocket guys. Merge to get them all!

What’s so special about Stick Defenders?
Merging stickmen is super fun! Building a strong army and blasting waves makes every fight a big thrill!

How do I use the wheel?
Click to spin the wheel after fights! You can win cool stuff like extra fighters or bombs to help your base!

Who made Stick Defenders?
TinyDobbins created it. They make fun stickman games like Merge Round Racers, with action for kids!

Is Stick Defenders multiplayer?
No, it’s single-player only. You fight alone, but it’s super fun to stop enemy waves!
