STICKMAN GAMES

Stickman games use funny stick figure people instead of regular characters! These simple black-line people can do amazing things like flips, fights, and crazy stunts. The simple look makes the games focus on fun movements and action instead of fancy graphics. Pizza Edition has stickman games with physics puzzles where you need to figure out how things move and fall. Our stickman games prove that something simple can still be super fun!
