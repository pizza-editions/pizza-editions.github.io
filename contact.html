Contact Us - Pizza Edition

We’d love to hear from you! At Pizza Edition, we’re all about making gaming fun and accessible with Browser Browser-based games like Choppy Orc, Race Survival, Gladihoppers, Poly Track, Tomb of the Mask, and more. Whether you have questions, feedback, want to request a game, or need help with our Service, here’s how you can reach us.

Get in Touch

Since we’re a small team focused on providing free games, we’ve made contacting us simple and straightforward. Use the options below to reach out, and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible!

  • Email Us: Send your questions, feedback, or game requests to our support email at rehandeveloper48@gmail.com. We’re here to help!
  • Visit Our Site: Check out more about us and explore our games at Pizza Edition.

Request a Game

Want to see a new game on Pizza Edition? We’re always looking to expand our collection of Browser games! To request a game, please email us at rehandeveloper48@gmail.com with the following details:

  • The name of the game you’d like to see added.
  • A brief description of the game (e.g., what type of game it is, why you love it).
  • If possible, a link to where the game can be found online (if it’s a Browser-based game).

We’ll review your request and do our best to add it to Pizza Edition if it fits our platform and is accessible for Browser play. Thank you for helping us grow our gaming library!

What We Can Help With

We’re happy to assist with a variety of topics, including:

  • Troubleshooting game issues (e.g., if a game isn’t loading properly).
  • Adding new games to Pizza Edition based on your requests.
  • Questions about our Terms and Conditions or Privacy Policy.
  • General feedback to help us improve your gaming experience.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries, including game requests, within 48-72 hours. Please be patient, as we’re a small team working to keep Pizza Edition running smoothly for everyone!

Thank you for choosing Pizza Edition to enjoy Browser gaming fun! We look forward to hearing from you and ensuring you have the best experience with our games.
